Project: NiO magnetic insulator study

Methods: PBEsol functional
         GGA+U with U=5.0 on Ni-d orbitals
         SOC with magnetization in z-direction
         HSE06 for accurate band gaps

Tasks: 1. Structure relaxation
       2. Self-consistent calculation
       3. Band structure along G-X-M-G-R-X
       4. DOS with projections on Ni:d and O:p
       5. Wannierization of Ni-d and O-p states
       6. Transport calculations with BoltzWann

Convergence: Test k-points from 6x6x6 to 12x12x12
             Test ENCUT 400-600 eV in steps of 50 eV

Goals: Investigate the electronic structure and transport properties of NiO.
       Focus on the band gap, magnetic moment, and effective mass.
       Compare PBEsol and HSE06 results.
